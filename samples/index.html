Background

A               The Partner and I-MED have entered into a separate services agreement (Services Agreement), on or about the date of this agreement, pursuant to which the Partner will, inter alia, provide to I-MED a range of support and maintenance services (Support Services) for equipment and systems located at I-MED premises. Some or all of such Support Services will be delivered to I-MED remotely.

B               To enable the Partner to provide the Support Services to I-MED remotely and in order to ensure that a secure method of connectivity is established between I-MED and the Partner (Business Purpose), I-MED has agreed to allow the Partner and each Partner Authorised Employee access to the I-MED information technology and communications network (I-MED Network) via the Network Connection, in accordance with this agreement.

The parties agree

1              Defined terms and interpretation

1.1          Definitions in the Dictionary

A term or expression starting with a capital letter which:

(a)           is defined in the Dictionary in Schedule 1 (Dictionary), has the meaning given to it in the Dictionary; and

(b)           is defined in the Corporations Act, but is not defined in the Dictionary, has the meaning given to it in the Corporations Act.

1.2          Interpretation

The interpretation clause in Schedule 1 (Dictionary) sets out rules of interpretation for this agreement.

2              Term

This agreement commences on the date it is signed by the party last to sign it and continues until the earlier to occur of:

(a)           the Expiry Date (if any, stated in Schedule 2 (Contract Details)) or earlier termination of this agreement;

(b)           the date upon which the Services Agreement expires or is terminated; or

(c)            the Business Purpose, in the opinion of I-MED, being no longer applicable.

3              Right to Use Network Connection

3.1          Grant of Access

I-MED grants permission to the Partner and each Partner Authorised Employee to use the Network Connection in order to access the I-MED Network on and subject to the terms of this agreement.

3.2          Use for Business Purpose only

The Partner and each Partner Authorised Employee must only use the Network Connection for the Business Purpose.

4              Partner Connection Request

(a)           The terms of this agreement are conditional upon and do not take effect until:

(i)             the Partner has completed and submitted to I-MED the form set out in Schedule 3 (Partner Connection Request); and

(ii)            the Partner Connection Request has been approved in writing by I-MED.

(b)           I-MED (as represented by I-MED’s GM Operations) may withhold its approval to any Partner Connection Request in its absolute discretion or make its approval of the Partner Connection Request subject to such conditions as it deems appropriate.

(c)           The Partner warrants that all information provided by the Partner in the Partner Connection Request is, at the time of submission, up to date and accurate.

5              Connectivity

(a)           I-MED will provide an encrypted VPN tunnel for the Network Connection, unless another connectivity method has been agreed and approved in writing by I-MED.

(b)           Encrypted VPN tunnels must be terminated on the Partner’s network.

6              Access to I-MED applications and devices

6.1          Limited access

The Partner and each Partner Authorised Employee must only access those specific applications and devices (e.g. hosts, routers, modalities) which have been specified in the Partner Connection Request and which have been approved in writing by I-MED.

7              I-MED Furnished Equipment

7.1          Provision of I-MED Furnished Equipment

(a)           I-MED may, in its sole discretion and in connection with the Partner’s access to the I-MED Network via the Network Connection, make available to the Partner such I-MED Furnished Equipment as may be specified in Schedule 2 (Contract Details).

(b)           In consideration of I-MED making the I-MED Furnished Equipment available, the Partner will pay I-MED the fee set out in Schedule 2 (Contract Details), yearly in advance. I-MED may increase this fee on an annual basis at I-MED’s sole discretion.

(c)            I-MED (or applicable third party) will retain title to any I-MED Furnished Equipment made available to the Partner and nothing in this agreement shall have the effect of nor is intended to, transfer title to any such I-MED Furnished Equipment to the Partner.

(d)           I-MED Furnished Equipment must be used solely by Partner Authorised Employees for the Business Purpose.

(e)           I-MED Furnished Equipment will only be configured for TCP/IP access.

7.2          Partner’s responsibilities for I-MED Furnished Equipment

(a)           The Partner must install I-MED Furnished Equipment only in its premises in a secure environment.

(b)           The Partner must not modify or access the configuration of the I-MED Furnished Equipment. If the Partner requires the I-MED Furnished Equipment to be modified or re-configured in any way for any reason, the Partner must request this in writing to I-MED with at least 2 weeks advance notice. Such request must specify the extent of any such re-configuration.

(c)            The Partner must not change or delete any passwords set on I-MED Furnished Equipment.

(d)           The Partner must provide reasonable access for I-MED to vary or audit the I-MED Furnished Equipment as required by I-MED.

(e)           The Partner will be responsible and liable for any damage caused by the Partner and the Partner Authorised Employees to the I-MED Furnished Equipment, other than fair wear and tear.

(f)             Upon termination or expiry of this agreement, the Partner must promptly return the I-MED Furnished Equipment to I-MED.

8              Network Security

8.1          Partner Authorised Employee access to Network Connection and I-MED Furnished Equipment

(a)           The Partner must ensure:

(i)             that only Partner Authorised Employees have access to the Network Connection and, if applicable, to the I-MED Network and, if applicable, to the I-MED Furnished Equipment;

(ii)            that each Partner Authorised Employee is made aware of and complies with the terms of this agreement; and

(iii)          that each Partner Authorised Employee must not disclose, share or permit other non-authorised employees of the Partner to use their password and/or other access credentials.

(b)           I-MED may require that any particular Partner Authorised Employee signs a deed poll confidentiality undertaking in such form as I-Med in its discretion determines.

(c)            The Partner represents and warrants that none of the Partner Authorised Employees represent a security risk to the I-MED Network.

(d)           Upon reasonable request by I-MED and at any time during the term of this agreement, the Partner will provide I-MED with any information reasonably necessary in order for I-MED to evaluate security issues, concerns or risks associated with access by any particular Partner Authorised Employee to the Network Connection and, if applicable, to any I-MED Furnished Equipment.

(e)           The Partner must promptly notify I-MED when any Partner Authorised Employee ceases to be employed by the Partner, or ceases to be authorised by the Partner to have access to the Network Connection and, if applicable, to the I-MED Furnished Equipment, or no longer requires access to the Network Connection and, if applicable, to the I-MED Furnished Equipment.

8.2          Security measures

(a)           The Partner must at its own cost and prior to any permitted use of and access to the Network Connection, establish, maintain and enforce effective security measures and controls in order to safeguard the Network Connection (and, if applicable, any I-MED Furnished Equipment) from unauthorised access or use, which are:

(i)             approved in writing by I-MED;

(ii)            consistent with Industry Best Practice, including without limitation in respect of virus protection practices in order to ensure that no Viruses are transmitted to the I-MED Network or I-MED Furnished Equipment (if applicable) as a result of the Partner’s access to and use of the Network Connection; and

(iii)          otherwise compliant with this agreement.

(b)           Partner acknowledges that its right to use the Network Connection and, if applicable, I-MED Furnished Equipment, is contingent at all times upon the Partner’s continued application of appropriate security measures in accordance with clause 8.2(a).

8.3          Reporting

The Partner must immediately notify I-MED and provide reasonable information as soon as it becomes aware of:

(a)           any failure by the Partner or any Partner Authorised Employee to comply with this agreement;

(b)           the occurrence of an unauthorised attempt to access the Network Connection and/or the I-MED Network;

(c)            any suspected security weakness in or security breach or attempted security breach of the I-MED Network; or

(d)           any actual or suspected misuse, interference, loss or unauthorised access, modification or disclosure of any data, including Personal Information, on the I-MED information technology and communications framework.

9              Audit

(a)           I-MED may upon reasonable notice to the Partner take all reasonable measures in order to audit or monitor compliance by the Partner with its obligations under this agreement.

(b)           To enable I-MED to audit or monitor compliance by the Partner with its obligations under this agreement, the Partner must fully co-operate with I-MED and make available to I-MED for inspection all systems, facilities, records, information and Partner Authorised Employees relating to the Partner’s access to and use of the Network Connection.

10         Change process

No change must be made to the Partner’s and each Partner Authorised Employee’s approved access to and use of the Network Connection and if applicable, the I-MED Furnished Equipment, unless such changes have first been approved in writing by I-MED.

11         Fees and costs associated with use of the Network Connection

Subject to clause 7.1(b), each party will be responsible for all of their own costs incurred by that party under this agreement, including, without limitation, costs for implementing and installing the Network Connection, phone, data and internet charges, telecommunications equipment, and personnel and maintenance agreements for maintaining the Network Connection.
